How Much Does Mediation Cost in Perth?

Pricing · ~3 min read

"What will this cost me?" is the first question most people ask — and unlike litigation, mediation has a refreshingly simple answer.

How fees work

  • Free first consultation. We assess whether mediation suits your dispute before you spend a cent.
  • Hourly billing at an industry-standard rate, confirmed clearly before anything begins. No hidden extras, no surprise line items.
  • Fees are split 50/50 between the parties. This isn't just fair — it's structural: the mediator is paid equally by both sides, so they work for neither. Neutrality is built into the invoice.

What you're actually paying for

A typical matter involves a one-hour private session for each party (so the mediator understands each side's real concerns in confidence), followed by joint mediation of at least two hours — extendable to four for complex matters. If both parties agree, sessions can repeat until the matter resolves or someone steps away.

So for many disputes, the total commitment is a handful of hours — compare that with litigation, where costs routinely run to tens of thousands of dollars and many months, with no guarantee of a better outcome.

Why hourly beats "fixed packages"

You pay for exactly the time your matter needs. Simple disputes finish fast and cheap; complex ones get the hours they deserve. And because both parties share the cost equally, there's a healthy incentive on everyone to come prepared and use the time well.

One tip that saves money

Get legal advice before mediation starts, so you know your bottom line and the true cost of the litigation alternative. Well-prepared parties settle faster — which directly reduces the hours on the clock.
