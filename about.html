About us

Resolving disputes with skill, neutrality and care

Harmony Mediation is built around AMDRAS nationally accredited mediation, helping businesses, workplaces and communities settle disputes calmly and confidentially — so you can resolve the matter without going to court.

AMDRAS accredited

Nationally accredited under AMDRAS (Australian Mediation and Dispute Resolution Accreditation Standards), meeting Australia's national standard for mediators.

200+ matters resolved

The practice has guided more than 200 mediations across commercial, workplace and community disputes. Our principal mediator is a University of Western Australia graduate.

Bilingual service

We work in both English and Chinese, so communication is clear and nothing gets lost in translation.

Our approach

The opposite of conflict isn't "winning" — it's resolution.

We believe most disputes don't have to end in mutual damage. As a neutral third party, we don't take sides — we help both parties understand the issues, narrow the gap and reach a workable agreement. Faster, cheaper and with dignity intact.

  • Neutral — impartial, focused only on a result both sides can accept.
  • Confidential — your privacy and reputation are protected throughout.
  • Practical — outcomes you can actually act on, not paper victories.

Note: in English we currently focus on commercial, workplace and community matters.
