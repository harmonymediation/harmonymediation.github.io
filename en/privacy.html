Privacy Policy & Disclaimer

Last updated: 2026 · Please have this reviewed for your jurisdiction (e.g. the Australian Privacy Act) before going live

1. Privacy Policy

We respect and protect your personal information. This explains what we collect, how we use it, and how we keep it safe.

What we collect

  • Information you provide through the enquiry form: your name, phone number, type of dispute and any brief description you choose to share;
  • Anonymous analytics data collected automatically (pages viewed, referral source, device type) to improve the website.

How we use it

  • To contact you, assess your situation and provide mediation consultations and services;
  • To improve our website and services.

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We only use or disclose it as necessary to provide our services or where required by law.

Protection & confidentiality

Mediation is strictly confidential. We apply reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information and retain it only as long as needed.

Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of the personal information we hold about you. To do so, contact us using the details in the footer.

2. Disclaimer

  • The content on this website (including Insights articles) is general information only and not legal advice for your specific situation;
  • Mediation is a voluntary process and no particular outcome is guaranteed; suitability depends on your individual circumstances;
  • For specific legal questions, please seek advice from a qualified lawyer;
  • Examples and figures in articles are illustrative.
